UI Animation

Animation of an App Interface

he objective of this project was to create an app and animate one of the interactions within the app. The scroll between different car models was the interaction chosen to animate. When animating an interaction it is important that it makes sense and isn't too much. So when animating this scroll I made sure that only the things that needed to move did. I assured they moved in a way that was clean and was the most logical.
