Andriod App Design

Wearable Technology

Pet owners need a way of keeping track of their pet’s well-being. The experience in this app is geared towards pet owners who want to better connect and take care of their pet. It allows them to track their pet’s whereabouts, steps, heart rate, sleep, and physical activity. Aside from being able to track your pet’s health, the app also notifies you when something is out of the normal, and gives you the option to forward that information to your vet.

Visual Design

The visual design of this app is designed in a way that is very easy to understand, which is what this specific user needs. The information and data that the wearable is collecting is displayed in a way that is easy to access. The colour palette that is used is meant to be associated with the idea of happiness and health. The chosen colours help promote an atmosphere that is upbeat, in hopes to make the experience fun and enjoyable. All of the typography consists of the font “Roboto”. This font was chosen because it is important to keep the consistency with other android applications

App Definition Statement

The purpose of this app is to help pet owners achieve a better relationship with their pet, and make sure their pets are healthy! This is an app that connects to a wearable technology, which in this case, is a collar that gathers information on the animal wearing it. This app is equipped with a variety of super cool features that make pet owners lives much easier.

Persona Overview

This app was created for pet lovers. People who really care about the health and happiness of their pets. It was designed perfectly for this persona because it allows you to check up on your pet. This app is every pet lovers dream!

Key Features

The first key feature of this app is the screen that shows all of the data that is collected from the animal’s collar. It allows the owner to easily sift through all of the information. Location is the second feature. It tracks where your pet is and displays it on a map. The final key feature is the alerts you receive when something regarding your pet’s health is out of the ordinary.
