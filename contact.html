About Me

My name is Hayley. I am a graphic designer from Barrie Ontario. I am currently attending Algonquin College in Ottawa. Currently I am in my last year of the graphic design program. I have also done some freelance work. When creating projects in school, I do what appeals to me as a designer. Freelancing has taught me how to appeal and create for others.

I have a very strong passion for branding, visual design, and motion. I also really enjoy a good challenge. I strive to make all of my designs creative regardless of what medium I am working with. I will work hours on end to find a creative solution that pushes the limits and I will love every minute of it!

I am looking forward to the journey that is ahead of me. Contact me if you want to be a part of it!
