Isometric Illustration

Isometric Illustrations are a way to display a visual representation of something three dimensional, in a two dimensional world. The grid uses lines that run parallel to one another. This allows for the illusion of depth. The use of different tints and shades of colours also really aids in creating the depth. This isometric illustration shows the city scene in the day time, and after dark.
