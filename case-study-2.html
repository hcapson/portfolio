Quote Book

Using Hierarchy to Create Expression

The theme behind this quote book is The Most Obnoxious Starbucks Orders. Everyone has stood in a Starbucks line one time or another and heard someone order and thought “what the hell did they just say” or “What language did they just speak”. This quote book has some of the craziest Starbucks orders to date and the decoded versions for those of us who are curious to understand the Starbucks regulars.
