The Tea Party Cafe

Visual Identity and Branding

The Tea Party Cafe’s logo reflects the quirky unique atmosphere costumers travel from all over to experience. The pictorial combines the two main selling points of the cafe, tea and the Mad Hatter theme. The Tea Party Cafe logo reinforces the quirky feel of the cafe, and the pride that the owners take in giving you an experience that is very different from all the rest.

Initial Sketches

The sketching process was the most important part in creating the cafe’s visual identity. It allowed many ideas to be flushed out and easily refined.

Versions of Logos

The structure of the hat makes use of negative space to display the quirkiness that the Cafe is known for. Visual-ly interesting negative space is used to create the teapot which helps establish the visual interest that the cafe gives to their customers.

The font choice helps costumers make the connection between the cafe’s theme and the Alice and Wonderland movie. The font very closely resembles the font used on the Alice and Wonderland movie poster.

Emphasis on the word cafe is achieved by making its font size the largest. Having the word “cafe” be the most prom-inent is important because it is the only piece of informa-tion that matters to the consumer.
